# Privacy Policy for BoostTime: Focus & Productivity

**Effective Date:** December 20, 2024
**Last Updated:** December 20, 2024

## Introduction

BoostTime ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the BoostTime mobile application (the "App").

By using BoostTime, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

BoostTime primarily stores data locally on your device using secure local storage. This includes:

#### Time Blocking Data
- **Time blocks:** Start time, end time, duration, title, description, and category
- **Completion status:** Whether time blocks are marked as completed
- **Progress tracking:** Real-time progress of active time blocks
- **Historical data:** Past time blocks for analytics and progress tracking

#### User Preferences and Settings
- **App settings:** Theme preference (light/dark), language selection (English/Bengali)
- **Daily goals:** Your personal 90-minute daily productivity target
- **Notification preferences:** Which types of notifications you want to receive
- **Location data:** City name for prayer time calculations (stored locally only)

#### Analytics and Usage Data
- **App usage patterns:** Which features you use most frequently
- **Goal achievement:** Daily, weekly, and monthly goal completion rates
- **Streak counters:** Consecutive days of goal achievement
- **Category preferences:** Which time block categories you use most

### Data We May Collect and Transmit

#### Firebase Analytics (Optional)
When you consent to analytics, we may collect:
- **App performance data:** Crash reports, app stability information
- **Usage statistics:** Feature usage, session duration (anonymized)
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version (anonymized)

#### Firebase Cloud Messaging (Notifications)
- **Device tokens:** For sending push notifications about time blocks and reminders
- **Notification interaction data:** Whether you open or dismiss notifications

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Financial information
- Contacts or social media data
- Precise location data (only city name for prayer times)
- Audio or video recordings
- Photos or camera access
- Microphone access

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data Processing
- **Time Management:** Creating, editing, and tracking your time blocks
- **Progress Analytics:** Generating insights about your productivity patterns
- **Goal Tracking:** Monitoring your daily, weekly, and monthly achievements
- **Prayer Integration:** Calculating prayer times based on your city
- **Personalization:** Customizing the app experience based on your preferences

### Cloud Services (When Enabled)
- **App Improvement:** Understanding how users interact with features to improve the app
- **Bug Fixes:** Identifying and fixing technical issues through crash reports
- **Push Notifications:** Sending reminders about upcoming time blocks

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your personal data is stored locally on your device using encrypted Hive database
- Data is not transmitted to external servers unless explicitly enabled
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time

### Cloud Storage
- Firebase services use industry-standard encryption
- Data transmitted to Firebase is anonymized and aggregated
- We do not store personal time block content on external servers

### Security Measures
- Local data encryption using Hive secure storage
- Network communications use HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is logged or stored in plain text
- Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Limited Data Sharing
We may share anonymized, aggregated data only in these circumstances:
- **Firebase Analytics:** Anonymous usage patterns to improve app performance
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our legal rights
- **App Store Analytics:** Basic download and usage statistics (anonymized)

### Third-Party Services
The app integrates with:
- **Firebase (Google):** For analytics and push notifications (optional)
- **Prayer Times API:** For calculating accurate prayer times (city-level only)

## Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Access:** You can view all your data within the app's settings and export features
- **Modification:** You can edit or delete any time blocks, goals, or preferences
- **Deletion:** You can clear all app data through the settings menu
- **Export:** You can export your data for backup or migration purposes

### Notification Control
- **Granular Controls:** Choose which types of notifications you want to receive
- **Opt-out:** Disable all notifications in the app settings or device settings
- **Frequency:** Control how often you receive reminder notifications

### Analytics Opt-out
- **Firebase Analytics:** Can be disabled in the app settings
- **Crash Reporting:** Can be disabled independently from analytics
- **Usage Statistics:** Local analytics remain private and never leave your device

## Children's Privacy

BoostTime is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our records.

## Islamic Values and Cultural Sensitivity

We respect Islamic values and cultural sensitivities in our data practices:
- **Prayer Time Privacy:** Prayer time calculations are done locally using your city information
- **Content Respect:** We do not analyze or process the content of your Islamic time blocks
- **Cultural Awareness:** Our app is designed with Islamic lifestyle considerations in mind

## Data Retention

### Local Data
- Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
- Uninstalling the app will remove all local data
- You can manually clear data through the app's settings

### Cloud Data
- Firebase analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies
- Anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 26 months
- You can request deletion of any cloud data by contacting us

## International Data Transfers

If you use BoostTime outside of Bangladesh:
- Local data remains on your device and is not transferred
- Firebase services may store anonymous data in Google's global infrastructure
- All transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations
