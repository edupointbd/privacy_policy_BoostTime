## Introduction

Muslim Focus: নামাজ সময় কুরআন ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle information when you use the Muslim Focus mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Most of your data is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted to external servers.

#### App Data

* Prayer schedules and preferences
* Quran reading progress
* Time tracking or productivity-related data (if applicable)
* User settings (theme, language, notifications)

---

### 2. Data Collected and Transmitted

We may collect limited data using third-party services:

#### Device or Other Identifiers

* Firebase Instance ID / Device Token
* Device type, operating system version

**Purpose:**

* Sending push notifications (e.g., prayer reminders)
* Improving app performance and stability

---

### 3. Location Data

#### Approximate Location (City-level)

* Used for:

  * Accurate prayer time calculations
* This data:

  * Is stored locally on your device
  * Is not shared with external servers

#### Precise Location

* We **do NOT collect or access precise location data** (GPS coordinates).

---

### 4. Analytics Data (Optional)

If enabled:

* App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
* Crash reports and diagnostics

**Collected via:** Firebase Analytics
**Data is anonymized and aggregated**

---

### 5. Notifications Data

* Device token (for push notifications)
* Notification interaction data (opened/dismissed)

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

* Name, email address, or phone number
* Financial or payment information
* Contacts or social media data
* Photos, videos, or audio recordings
* Precise GPS location
